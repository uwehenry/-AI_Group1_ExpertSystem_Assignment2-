Common Diseases

Maize Leaf Blight

Symptoms: Long, brown lesions on leaves

Treatment: Apply fungicide containing azoxystrobin

Coffee Leaf Rust

Symptoms: Orange-yellow powdery spots

Treatment: Copper-based fungicides

Treatment Guidelines

  • 1. Identify the disease accurately using our diagnostic tool
  • 2. Follow recommended treatment protocol
  • 3. Apply treatments early morning or late afternoon
  • 4. Monitor crop response for 7-14 days
  • 5. Seek expert advice if symptoms persist
  • 6. Keep records of treatments and results for future reference
